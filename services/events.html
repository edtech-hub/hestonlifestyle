Events Photography

Conferences, launches, galas, parties and brand activations — captured beautifully.

About this service

Bespoke events photography in London

Live events demand a calm photographer who reads a room, anticipates moments and works invisibly. We cover corporate conferences, product launches, charity galas, awards ceremonies, private parties and brand activations across London and beyond.

You receive a mix of essentials — the room, the speakers, the brand moments, the people — and the atmospheric, candid frames that bring the event back to life when you share it.

Same-day or next-morning hero-image delivery is available for press & social, with the full gallery to follow within 5 working days.

What's included

  • Conferences, launches & awards
  • Private parties & celebrations
  • Brand activations & pop-ups
  • Same-day hero-image delivery option
  • Discreet, calm on-the-floor presence
  • Multi-photographer coverage available
