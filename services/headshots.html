Headshots

About this service

Bespoke headshots in London

Looking to impress potential clients and employers? It pays to have a professional image to promote your career — and we make great headshots simple.

Choose your background and style of shoot. We will make sure your pose is perfect, so we capture the real you. Or get us to visit your offices for a more personalised shoot.

You receive a curated selection of images to choose from, retouched to make sure you look great on LinkedIn, your website and social media. Full colour and black & white versions of every final shot.

What's included

  • Studio or on-site at your office
  • Individuals, teams or full company rosters
  • Mobile lighting + portable backdrop available
  • Online, password-protected gallery for selection
  • Full retouching of selected frames
  • High-res files for print, low-res for web/social
